Education has many objectives, including: All-round development: Education should help students develop physically, mentally, socially, and aesthetically. Character building: Education should help students develop good personalities and become responsible citizens. Moral values: Education should help students develop moral values. Equity and inclusion: Education should create a learning environment that celebrates diversity, promotes social justice, and is responsive to individual needs. Effective communication: Education should help students develop the ability to clearly express themselves, listen actively, and articulate ideas.
